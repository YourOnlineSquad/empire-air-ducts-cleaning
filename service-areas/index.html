San Bernardino County Ventilation FAQ

How do Santa Ana winds affect my ductwork in San Bernardino?

The high-velocity Santa Ana winds blow massive amounts of fine desert dust, pollen, and debris into the crevices of homes. This debris is quickly pulled into the HVAC return vents, clogging the system and reducing your indoor air quality. Routine air duct cleaning is crucial in this region.

Do you clean massive industrial warehouse ducts in Ontario and Fontana?

Yes. We utilize high-capacity, heavy-duty negative air extraction systems specifically designed to handle the massive cubic footage and exposed spiral ductwork common in San Bernardino County warehouses and logistics facilities.

Can dirty ducts actually affect my family's allergies?

Yes. If dust, pollen, and pet dander are trapped in your ducts, your HVAC system redistributes them into the air you breathe up to 7 times a day, frequently triggering asthma and allergy symptoms. Extracting the buildup removes the source of the irritation.
