Riverside County Ventilation FAQ

How often should vents be cleaned in Riverside County?

Due to the intense heat, high AC usage, and dry desert dust in Riverside County, we recommend air duct cleaning every 3 to 5 years. Dryer vents should be cleared annually to prevent serious fire hazards.

Can cleaning my ducts help with Riverside wildfire ash?

Yes. During fire season, microscopic ash infiltrates home HVAC systems and settles in the ductwork. Professional negative air extraction removes this toxic ash, preventing it from recirculating into your breathable air and irritating asthma and allergies.

What are the signs my dryer vent is clogged in my Temecula home?

If your clothes take more than one cycle to dry, the laundry room gets excessively hot, or you smell a burning lint odor, your dryer vent is dangerously clogged and requires immediate professional clearing.
