NADCA Guidelines

The Science of Source Removal

At Empire Air Ducts Cleaning we adhere strictly to the National Air Duct Cleaners Association standard known as Source Removal. This is the only scientifically proven method to safely and permanently remove contaminants from an HVAC system without pushing them back into your breathable airspace. We have done this many times in Murrieta and understand exactly what local systems need to run efficiently.

Step 1 System Isolation and Negative Pressure
We begin by sealing off your supply and return registers. We then attach a massive commercial grade vacuum unit equipped with hospital grade HEPA filters directly to your main HVAC trunk line. This places the entire system under intense negative pressure creating a powerful one way airflow out of your house.

Step 2 Pneumatic Agitation
While the system is under this intense suction we introduce specialized pneumatic tools such as compressed air whips and rotary brushes deep into the ductwork. These tools safely but aggressively scrub the interior walls of your flex and rigid metal ducts.

Step 3 Safe Eradication
As decades of caked on dust, dead skin cells, construction debris, and pet dander are dislodged by the whips, the negative pressure instantly pulls the hazardous particulate matter out of your home and directly into our contained filtration system. Without proper cleaning this material remains trapped in your home forever.

Air Duct Extraction FAQ

In depth technical answers to help you make an informed decision regarding your property ventilation health.

Will the negative air pressure vacuum damage my flexible ductwork?

No. Our advanced extraction equipment is meticulously calibrated to safely handle all types of residential and commercial ventilation in Murrieta. We have done this many times in Murrieta and whether you have modern flexible ducting, traditional rigid sheet metal, or older fiberglass duct board, our pneumatic whips are designed to be gentle on the duct walls while remaining highly effective at dislodging built up debris.

Why should I avoid hiring a budget carpet cleaner to clean my air ducts?

Many budget companies utilize standard shop vacuums that lack the raw cubic feet per minute required to place an entire home HVAC system under true negative pressure. Without this pressure and commercial HEPA filtration these budget services often just agitate the dust and blow it directly out of your vents and into your living space making your indoor air quality significantly worse than before.

What is HVAC sanitization and do I actually need it?

HVAC sanitization is an optional add on that involves fogging an EPA registered botanical antimicrobial solution throughout your freshly cleaned ductwork. We highly recommend this service if your system has previously suffered from roof leaks or moisture issues, has a lingering musty odor, or if you are moving into a previously occupied home and want to permanently neutralize existing biological contaminants left by previous owners or pets.

How long does a comprehensive extraction take?

Because we do not cut corners a standard extraction for a single family home in Murrieta approximately 1500 to 2500 square feet with one central HVAC system typically takes between two to four hours. Larger commercial properties or multi story homes with dual HVAC units will naturally require additional time to ensure every branch line is fully cleared to our strict standards.
