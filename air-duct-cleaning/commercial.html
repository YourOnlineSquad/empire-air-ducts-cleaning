Commercial IAQ FAQ

Technical answers for facility managers and commercial property owners in Murrieta CA.

Do you offer after hours commercial duct cleaning in Murrieta?

Yes. We know that shutting down a busy retail store or medical facility during operating hours is impossible. Our Murrieta based crews offer flexible after hours, overnight, and weekend scheduling to complete the extraction with zero disruption to your daily operations.

Can you handle large scale warehouse or industrial HVAC systems?

Absolutely. Standard shop vacuums are useless on commercial properties. Our Riverside County fleet is equipped with gas powered, heavy duty negative air extraction units specifically engineered to pull massive CFMs from high tonnage rooftop units and extensive spiral ductwork.

Will commercial duct cleaning actually lower my facility overhead costs?

Yes. When your commercial evaporator coils and blower motors are choked with manufacturing dust, the system must work significantly harder to push air. This spikes your monthly energy bills. A professional deep extraction restores the original airflow specifications, which directly lowers energy consumption and extends the lifespan of expensive HVAC equipment.

How do I know the duct cleaning was actually effective?

We believe in total transparency for our commercial clients. Before we begin, we conduct a thorough camera inspection of the duct interior. Once the pneumatic extraction is complete, we provide post cleaning verification photos so you can visibly see the bare metal results and confirm the job was done correctly.

Does cleaning the ductwork help with Sick Building Syndrome?

Yes, it is often the primary solution. If employees are constantly complaining of headaches, seasonal allergies, or chronic respiratory fatigue while inside the building, the HVAC system is likely recirculating trapped mold spores, VOCs, and dust. Eradicating these contaminants from the ductwork dramatically improves indoor air quality and workforce productivity.
