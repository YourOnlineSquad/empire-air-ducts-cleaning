Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to common ventilation concerns in Southern California.

How often should air ducts be cleaned in Murrieta? +

For most homes, we recommend air duct cleaning every 3 to 5 years. However, properties with multiple pets, recent remodeling dust, or residents sensitive to allergens may require more frequent cleanings to maintain optimal indoor air quality.

What are the signs my dryer vent is dangerously clogged? +

Warning signs include laundry taking longer than one cycle to dry, the dryer exterior becoming abnormally hot, a burning lint odor in the laundry room, and visible lint blocking the outside exhaust flap.

Does dirty ductwork really affect my allergies? +

Yes. HVAC systems continuously cycle air. If dust mites, pollen, pet dander, or mold spores are trapped in your ducts, they are redistributed into your living space up to 7 times a day, often triggering asthma and allergy symptoms.

Do you service commercial properties? +

Absolutely. We provide full commercial air duct cleaning for offices and warehouses, as well as specialized grease removal for restaurant hoods to ensure fire code compliance.
